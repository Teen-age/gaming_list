Honkai:Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based role-playing game developed and published by HoYoverse. It was officially released on April 26, 2023.
The game blends sci-fi exploration with anime-inspired storytelling, set across various unique worlds connected by the Astral Express train.

In Honkai: Star Rail, players assemble a team of characters, each with distinct abilities and roles, to battle enemies in strategic, turn-based combat.
The game focuses on character progression, story-driven quests, and uncovering the mysteries of the universe while traveling from planet to planet.
